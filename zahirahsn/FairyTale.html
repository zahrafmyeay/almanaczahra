Fairy Tale

Cinderella

In Cinderella. she combines the interior with the French eighteen-century history and fantasy provincial style. Baroque influenced the castle with prospered architecture in Europe, especially in France during the reign of Louis XIV(Allan 1999). The design shows the detail of luxury from the shiny floor to exquisite furniture (Isojärvi 2022).

Peter Pan

In the Peter Pan animation, Mary enhanced the visual setting of Victorian in the late nineteenth century. Blair uses an odd colour scheme, a pale green, for the sky (Canemaker 2014). Mary broadened the colour palette and language of visuals in the Disney production as well as introduced Disney to modern art (Adams, 2017)

Alice In Wonderland

Mary’s work was very experimental. She used the element of Tenniel's illustration (Victoria and Albert Museum 2021) in the wood blocks and tried to explore the text from her representation of Wonderland. She overcame this challenge by visiting Long Island in New York City. She immersed herself in the story and created the character of Alice. She designed beautiful visuals for each of the scenes (Holt 2019).
